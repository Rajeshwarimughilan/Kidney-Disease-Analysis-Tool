NephroScan

Age should be between 18 and 65.
A normal reading is typically 120/80 mmHg.
A normal fasting blood sugar level is 70-99 mg/dL.
A normal creatinine level is 0.6-1.2 mg/dL.
A normal urea level is 7-20 mg/dL.
Normal hemoglobin levels: Men: 13.8-17.2 g/dL, Women: 12.1-15.1 g/dL.
A normal white blood cell count is 4,500-11,000 cells/mcL.
Normal cholesterol level is less than 200 mg/dL.
